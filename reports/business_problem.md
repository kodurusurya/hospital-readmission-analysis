
# Business Problem

Hospital readmissions within 30 days are a major challenge for healthcare organizations because they can increase costs, reduce bed availability, and indicate gaps in care coordination.

This project analyzes hospital encounter data for diabetic patients to identify factors associated with readmission risk. The goal is to help healthcare stakeholders better understand high-risk patient groups and support improved discharge planning.

## Objective

The objective of this project is to answer the following question:

Which patient, clinical, and hospital utilization factors are associated with 30-day readmissions?

## Stakeholder Scenario

A hospital leadership team wants to reduce avoidable readmissions. As a healthcare data analyst, the goal is to analyze historical encounter data and provide insights that can support targeted follow-up care, better discharge planning, and improved resource allocation.
